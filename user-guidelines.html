User Guidelines

Complete step-by-step guide for KSFP platform users

School Registration & Setup

Complete guide for schools to register and configure their accounts

1

Access School Login Portal

Navigate to the KSFP platform and click on "School Login" from the main landing page. This will take you to the dedicated school authentication portal.

School Login Portal Screenshot

2

Register Your School

If your school is not yet registered, click "Register New School". Provide your school's official details including name, location, registration number, and contact information. Upload required documents for verification.

Important

Ensure all information matches your official school registration documents. Incorrect information may delay approval.

3

Verify School Details

After registration, your school will undergo verification by KSFP administrators. This process typically takes 24-48 hours. You'll receive email notifications about the status.

4

Set Up Fee Structure

Once verified, log in to your school dashboard. The system will automatically assign the correct fee structure based on your school type and location (CBC Senior Schools: KSh 52,600/term, constituency rates apply).

5

Configure Payment Methods

Set up your preferred payment collection methods including M-Pesa, bank transfers, and cash payments. Configure payment notifications and receipts.

6

Manage Student Records

Add student information, assign classes, and set up fee payment schedules. The system will automatically generate invoices and track payment status.

Fee Collection & Management

How to collect and manage school fees effectively

1

Generate Fee Invoices

Use the dashboard to generate fee invoices for individual students or entire classes. Invoices include QR codes for easy mobile payment.

2

Accept Payments

Accept payments through multiple channels: M-Pesa, bank transfers, card payments, and cash. The system automatically updates payment status and sends receipts.

3

Track Payment History

Monitor all payments, outstanding balances, and generate detailed financial reports for your school administration.

Parent Registration & Access

How parents can register and access their children's fee information

1

Access Parent Portal

Visit the KSFP platform and click "Parent/Cybercafe Login". This portal is designed for parents and cybercafe operators to access fee information.

2

Register as Parent

Create an account using your phone number or email. Provide your details and link your children to their respective schools.

3

Link Student Accounts

Add your children's admission numbers or use the school's registration code to link their accounts to your parent dashboard.

4

View Fee Information

Access detailed fee breakdowns, payment history, outstanding balances, and upcoming payment deadlines for each child.

5

Make Payments

Pay school fees securely using M-Pesa, card payments, or bank transfers. Receive instant confirmation and receipts.

Mobile Payment Guide

Step-by-step guide for mobile money payments

1

Select Payment Method

Choose M-Pesa as your payment method from the payment options in your parent dashboard.

2

Enter Payment Details

Enter the exact amount shown on the invoice. The system will display the school's M-Pesa paybill number.

3

Complete M-Pesa Transaction

On your phone, go to M-Pesa > Lipa na M-Pesa > Pay Bill. Enter the business number and account number provided.

4

Confirm Payment

After successful payment, return to the KSFP platform and click "Confirm Payment" to update your payment status.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register my school on KSFP?
Visit the school login portal and click "Register New School". Fill in your school's official details, upload required documents, and submit for verification. The process typically takes 24-48 hours.
What are the current fee rates for CBC Senior Schools?
CBC Senior Schools are charged KSh 52,600 per term as per government guidelines. Free day schools have no fees, while constituency schools like Kiharu (KSh 500/term) and Mumias East (KSh 1,300/term) have special rates.
How can parents pay school fees?
Parents can pay through M-Pesa, bank transfers, card payments, or cash at designated collection points. The system supports multiple payment methods for convenience.
What should I do if I forget my password?
Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. For administrators, contact the system administrator at noreply@kssfk.com. For schools and parents, follow the password reset instructions sent to your registered email.
How do I update my school's fee structure?
School administrators can update fee structures through their dashboard. For government-regulated changes, contact KSFP administrators who will implement system-wide updates.
Is the platform secure for financial transactions?
Yes, KSFP uses bank-level security with SSL encryption, secure payment gateways, and complies with Kenyan data protection regulations. All transactions are monitored and logged.
How do I generate financial reports?
Schools can generate reports from their dashboard under "Reports" section. Administrators have access to system-wide analytics and can export data in various formats.
What if I encounter technical issues?
Contact our support team at noreply@kssfk.com or call +254 768 331 888. Include screenshots and detailed descriptions of the issue for faster resolution.
