# Privacy Policy

**Kenya School Fee Platform (KSFP)**
Effective Date: January 2026
Version: 1.0 (Enterprise)

---

## 1. INTRODUCTION

Kenya School Fee Platform ("KSFP") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains:
- What data we collect
- Why we collect it
- How we protect it
- Your rights regarding your data

---

## 2. DATA WE COLLECT

### 2.1 From Parents
We collect:
- **Account Information:** Name, email, phone, location
- **Payment Data:** Transaction details, amounts, dates (NOT credit card numbers)
- **Search Activity:** Schools viewed, ratings submitted
- **Communications:** Messages to schools or support
- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, location

### 2.2 From Schools
We collect:
- **Institutional Data:** Name, location, contacts, history
- **Academic Information:** Performance metrics, accreditation
- **Financial Data:** Fee structures, payment information
- **Vacancy Information:** Classes, capacity, enrollment
- **Uploaded Files:** Prospectuses, documents, images
- **Usage Data:** Logins, profile updates, responses

### 2.3 Automatically Collected
- **Log Data:** IP address, browser, access times
- **Cookies:** Session and preference tracking
- **Analytics:** Platform usage patterns

---

## 3. HOW WE USE DATA

### 3.1 Service Operations
We use data to:
- Process payments and generate receipts
- Calculate star ratings
- Display school information to parents
- Verify data accuracy
- Support customer service
- Prevent fraud

### 3.2 Legal & Safety
We use data to:
- Comply with Kenya laws
- Enforce Terms and Conditions
- Investigate violations
- Detect fraudulent activity
- Protect platform security
- Respond to government requests

### 3.3 Improvement & Analytics
We use data to:
- Improve platform features
- Analyze usage patterns
- Research education trends
- Generate anonymized reports
- Train AI systems (anonymized only)

### 3.4 Marketing
We use data to:
- Send platform updates (opt-out available)
- Notify of new features
- Provide system alerts
- Request feedback

---

## 4. DATA PROTECTION

### 4.1 Security Measures
KSFP implements:
- **Encryption:** AES-256 for data at rest, TLS 1.2+ in transit
- **Firewalls:** Network and application firewalls
- **Access Control:** Role-based access, multi-factor authentication
- **Backups:** Daily encrypted backups with disaster recovery
- **Monitoring:** 24/7 security monitoring and intrusion detection

### 4.2 ACID Compliance
All data transactions are:
- **Atomic:** All-or-nothing operations
- **Consistent:** Data always valid
- **Isolated:** No concurrent conflicts
- **Durable:** Permanent immutable records

### 4.3 Immutable Logs
All sensitive operations logged with:
- Tamper-detection (SHA-256 hash chains)
- Timestamp verification
- Sequential numbering
- Permanent audit trail

---

## 5. DATA SHARING

### 5.1 Third Parties
We share data ONLY with:
- **Payment Processors:** Essential for transaction processing (PCI-DSS compliant)
- **Legal Authorities:** When required by law (with notice when possible)
- **Service Providers:** Cloud hosting, email, analytics (under contracts)

### 5.2 School Information
School information (name, fees, vacancies) is PUBLIC by design:
- Visible to all parents
- Used for platform rating
- Subject to school's terms

### 5.3 NOT Shared
We never sell, rent, or share for profit:
- Parent personal data
- Payment details
- Behavioral data
- Contact information

---

## 6. DATA RETENTION

### 6.1 Retention Periods
- **Payment Records:** 7 years (tax & legal requirement)
- **Receipts:** 5 years (for verification)
- **Parent Account:** Until deletion + 1 year backup
- **School Account:** Until deletion + 1 year backup
- **Access Logs:** 90 days
- **Audit Logs:** Permanent (immutable)

### 6.2 Deletion
You may request deletion of:
- Account information
- Uploaded documents
- Activity history

We will delete within 30 days, except:
- Data required by law (retained)
- Anonymized data (retained)
- Backup copies (deleted within 1 year)

## 7. USER RIGHTS

### 7.1 Data Access
You have the right to:
- Request copy of your data
- See how data is used
- Understand automated decisions
- Get explanations of processing

Request by emailing: privacy@ksfp.ac.ke

### 7.2 Data Correction
You may:
- Update your profile
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request corrections for school data

### 7.3 Data Deletion
You may request deletion of:
- Your account
- Your personal data
- Your uploaded content

**Legal Holds:** Data needed for legal disputes retained.

### 7.4 Data Portability
You may request:
- Your data in machine-readable format
- Transfer to another service
- Backup of all your records

### 7.5 Opt-Out
You may opt-out of:
- Marketing communications
- Non-essential analytics
- Behavioral tracking

## 8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

- Parents manage student information
- Schools responsible for student privacy

## 9. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS
