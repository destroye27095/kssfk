## 2. SERVICE DESCRIPTION

KSFP is a digital platform that:
- Provides school discovery and information services
- Facilitates fee transparency and vacancy management
- Processes parent payments and generates receipts
- Implements a star rating system based on school information accuracy

**IMPORTANT:** KSFP is a platform only. KSFP does not:
- Guarantee school admission or enrollment
- Endorse any school or institution
- Provide educational advice
- Accept liability for school conduct or performance

---

## 3. SCHOOL RESPONSIBILITIES

### 3.1 Data Accuracy
Schools uploading data to KSFP warrant that:
- All information is accurate and current
- Vacancy numbers match actual capacity
- Fees and costs are correctly stated
- Academic information is verified and honest
- Documents uploaded are authentic

### 3.2 Penalty for False Information
Schools providing false, misleading, or fraudulent information may face:
- Rating reduction (0.5-2 stars)
- Fee increase penalty (5%-50% added charges)
- Account suspension or termination
- Legal action for fraud

**20% Penalty Enforcement:** Schools detected with false information will have fees INCREASE by 20% for one year.

### 3.3 Data Updates
Schools must:
- Update information within 7 days of changes
- Remove outdated or incorrect data
- Respond to parent inquiries promptly
- Cooperate with KSFP audits

## 5. STAR RATING SYSTEM

KSFP implements a 5-star rating calculated using:
- Fee Transparency (25%)
- Academic Results (25%)
- Upload Accuracy (20%)
- Vacancy Honesty (15%)
- Parent Feedback (10%)

**Rating Penalties:**
- Mild violations: -0.5 stars
- Moderate violations: -1.0 stars
- Severe violations: -1.5 stars

Ratings are locked annually to prevent gaming.

### 6.1 Payment Processing
- All payments processed through secure gateway
- KSFP retains no payment details (PCI compliant)

### 6.2 Refunds
- Refunds processed within 7-14 business days

### 6.3 ACID Compliance

- Atomicity: All operations succeed or none

## 8. ENFORCEMENT AND PENALTIES

### 8.1 Violation Detection
KSFP uses automated systems to detect:
- Duplicate files or plagiarism
- Inconsistent capacity/enrollment data
- Suspicious fee changes
- Contradictory information
- Pattern-based fraud indicators

### 8.2 Penalty Process
1. Violation detected automatically or reported
2. School notified of flags and evidence
3. School given 7 days to respond or correct
4. Penalty applied if unresolved
5. Appeal process available within 14 days

### 8.3 Penalty Types
- **Rating Reduction:** 0.5-2 star decrease
- **Fee Penalty:** 20% increase for one year
- **Suspension:** Temporary delisting (7-30 days)
- **Termination:** Permanent removal for severe fraud

### 8.4 Appeal Process
Schools may appeal penalties within 14 days by:
- Submitting written explanation
- Providing corrected information
- Offering evidence of accuracy
- KSFP reviews and decides within 7 days
