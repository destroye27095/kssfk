What is KSFP?

KSFP is a platform that helps parents and schools manage school fee payments and records. We provide interfaces for initiating payments, viewing receipts, and managing uploads.

Payments

Payments can be made via supported providers (online) or by paying into a KCB Paybill for manual processing.
KCB Paybill: 522522 — Account: 0768331888

After you make a KCB Paybill payment, keep your receipt and follow the platform instructions to mark the invoice as paid or upload proof if requested by the school.

Privacy & Data

We collect your name, phone number and email to authenticate you and send payment notifications. We store only what we need and do not sell your data.

Your responsibilities

  • Provide accurate contact and payment information.
  • Keep your login secure and do not share passwords.
  • Keep receipts for any manual payments (e.g., Paybill).

Disputes & Liability

KSFP acts as the platform. Schools and payment providers are responsible for issuing final receipts. If there's a dispute about a payment, contact the school and provide your receipt; we will assist where appropriate.
