About KSFP

Kenya School Fee Platform (KSFP) is a national-scale digital platform designed to revolutionize how parents and schools interact. We provide transparent school information, secure payment processing, and a trust-based rating system to help families make informed education decisions.

Built with enterprise-grade architecture, KSFP ensures financial security, data integrity, and accountability through ACID-compliant transactions, immutable audit trails, and comprehensive legal protection.
